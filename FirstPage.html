The Catastrophe strikes!

In Dec 2019 a catastrophe struck, which slowly spread across the globe. Who knew back then that this would turn out to be one of the most disastrous Pandemics of all times.

The outbreak of 2019 novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) was first reported on December 31, 2019, in Wuhan, China, sending the shivers around the globe. By March 2020 , the disease had been declared as a pandemic by WHO (World Health organization) and a public health emergency of international concern, theÂ highest level of alarm under international law. Little did the world know that life would literally come to a standstill.

Over the next couple of months , the scientific community struggled to detect the source of the virus , while the medical community globally ,grappled to find the cure in existing and available medical therapies.

What is this virus?

In the term COVID 19, 'COVI' stands for coronavirus which are basically a group of viruses with crown shaped parts bulging out. 'D' stands for diseases and 19 is 2019 , the year the first case was reported.

This virus behaves in a very different way. Some people may have the symptoms and some may not. Some common symptoms are:
- Fever
- Fatigue
- Dry cough

These symptoms are the early symptoms. The symptoms that later on come after the virus starts attacking rest of the body and blood stream are:
- Aches and pains
- Runny nose
- Sore Throat
- Shortness of breath
- Diarrhea

There is currently no proven vaccine to be affective against this virus however, it may be cured by medical care. It usually takes around 14 - 17 days to fully be cured from the virus however, in some severe cases, it might even last for more time.

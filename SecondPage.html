- Drinking alcohol help prevent COVID-19
- COVID-19 is transmitted in cold climate and not in hot and humid climate
- Spraying alcohol or chlorine on your body kill the virus inside
- Eating garlic prevent COVID-19
- Pneumonia vaccine prevent COVID-19
